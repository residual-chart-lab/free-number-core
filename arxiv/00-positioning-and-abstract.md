# arXiv manuscript positioning — working draft

## Working title

**Exact Reconstruction under Noncommutative Quaternionic Compression**

Subtitle candidate:

**Finite-Grade Contextual Responses, Recursive Decoding, and Observability Depth**

Project label may appear in the author note or final subtitle as **Free Numbers Core v1**, but the searchable mathematical mechanism leads the title.

## Working abstract

Let \(V=\operatorname{Im}\mathbb H\), \(B_n=V^{\otimes n}\), and let
\[
m_n(a_1|\cdots|a_n)=a_n\cdots a_1
\]
be reversed quaternionic compression. The maps \(m_n\) are deliberately non-injective: distinct boundary tensors may have the same quaternionic value. We show that the distinctions erased at value level remain exactly recoverable from a canonical family of contextual probe responses. Inserting one imaginary-quaternion probe into every internal gap defines
\[
A_n:B_n\longrightarrow \operatorname{Hom}(V^{\otimes(n-1)},\mathbb H).
\]
A single twelve-dimensional local map
\[
\Theta:V\otimes\mathbb H\longrightarrow\operatorname{Hom}(V,\mathbb H)
\]
is an isomorphism, with an explicit inverse and determinant \(256\) in the standard basis. Iterating this local inverse removes one outer boundary layer at a time and proves that \(A_n\) is injective for every finite \(n\). Consequently every nonzero finite state is detected by probe depth at most \(n-1\). The first-detection depth defines a nontrivial observability filtration that separates structures identified by ordinary compression and can split equal representation types according to factor origin. Exact-response coordinates also carry a natural unital associative graded product induced by boundary concatenation. The resulting construction is a finite, explicit model in which coarse noncommutative compression coexists with exact contextual recoverability. It suggests a template for query-addressable memory, active sensing, structured state reconstruction, and provenance-preserving computation, without asserting an efficiency or noise-stability theorem.

## Central claim

> We do not claim that every ingredient is new in isolation. We claim that the combined structure closes.

The combined closed structure consists of:

1. deliberately non-injective reversed quaternionic compression;
2. canonical all-gap contextual probing;
3. one explicit local inverse reused at every finite grade;
4. exact finite reconstruction;
5. a first-detection-depth filtration;
6. nonzero zero-valued states with delayed contextual visibility;
7. an associative graded product on exact-response coordinates.

## Strong contribution statement

The contribution is not another decoder for an injective encoding. The compressed quaternionic value is genuinely many-to-one. Exact recoverability appears only after retaining structured contextual responses, and the reconstruction is driven by one finite local gate rather than by a separate global inverse at each tensor grade.

Output equality is therefore strictly weaker than state equality, while state equality remains finitely decidable in exact-response coordinates.

## Known mathematical neighborhoods

The manuscript must acknowledge established work on:

- state-space realization, controllability, and observability;
- noncommutative rational formal power-series realizations;
- weighted automata and Hankel-rank realization;
- tensor and quantum-state tomography;
- quaternionic algebra and representation theory;
- active sensing and structured inverse problems.

These subjects supply important analogies and techniques. They do not, merely by their existence, establish the specific quaternionic all-gap response architecture proved here.

## Unresolved priority boundary

Until the literature audit is complete, the manuscript must not claim:

- that no equivalent construction exists under different terminology;
- that the architecture is the first in coding theory, realization theory, or tomography;
- that every component is individually novel;
- that the framework improves storage complexity, probe complexity, or numerical stability;
- that it already gives an error-correcting code or production memory system.

The defensible strong statement is:

> To the best of our current knowledge, the particular closed combination of reversed quaternionic compression, all-gap insertion responses, an explicit recursive local decoder, finite observability depth, and associative exact-response composition has not yet been located in the adjacent literature reviewed here.

This sentence remains provisional until the audit is documented source by source.

## Application signal

The paper should expose, but not oversell, the following architectural possibility:

> Coarse summaries may be kept inexpensive while distinctions erased by compression remain recoverable through finite, depth-controlled contextual queries.

Potential interfaces include query-addressable memory, active sensing, structured hidden-state recovery, provenance-preserving summaries, and systems that defer exact reconstruction until a collision or ambiguity occurs.

No claim of practical memory savings is made in Core v1. Since \(\dim B_n=\dim\mathfrak A_n=3^n\), an efficiency theorem would require additional assumptions such as sparsity, shallow typical detection depth, generated rather than stored responses, or selective query recovery.

## Non-negotiable scope boundary

This manuscript contains no Time Engine, consciousness interpretation, physical ontology, AI architecture theorem, or universal classification of Free Numbers models. Such material may appear only as clearly labelled future interfaces, and preferably not in the first submission.
