# Standalone Independence Audit — Conditioned Zero

**Status:** local independence audit, not a completion proof for the full Free Numbers theory  
**Branch:** `core-reconstruction`  
**Audits:** `core/02-conditioned-zero.md`

## 0. Audit question

The strict test is:

> After removing all temporal, causal, conscious, ethical, and originating interpretive language, does a nontrivial mathematical phenomenon remain that can be defined and realized without the Time Engine, the Y-axis theory, or the quaternionic boundary-word model?

The audit distinguishes four levels.

1. **Lexical independence:** the mathematical statement does not require originating vocabulary.
2. **Definitional independence:** its primitives and laws can be stated without originating ontology.
3. **Model independence:** at least one concrete model exists that does not use the originating theory.
4. **Full-theory independence:** composition, equality, residual depth, and closure are all established without the originating theory.

This note audits the conditioned-zero birth kernel only. It does not claim level 4 for the full Free Numbers program.

---

## 1. Mathematical residue after interpretation is removed

The irreducible local statement is:

Let

\[
m_n:B_n\to A
\]

be a compression map and let admissible probes produce a response profile \(J\). Then there may exist

\[
z,z'\in B_n
\]

such that

\[
m_n(z)=m_n(z')=0_A
\]

while

\[
J(z)\ne J(z').
\]

Equivalently, the zero fiber

\[
Z_n=m_n^{-1}(0_A)
\]

need not be observationally trivial.

This statement contains no temporal primitive. It requires only:

- representatives;
- a compressed value object;
- a non-injective compression map;
- admissible contextual observations;
- a response distinction inside one compression fiber.

Therefore the conditioned-zero phenomenon is definitionally independent of its originating interpretation.

## 2. Minimal non-temporal witness model

Definitional independence alone is not enough. A model unrelated to the originating interpretation must exist.

Take

\[
A=\mathbb R,
\qquad
B_1=B_2=\mathbb R^2.
\]

Define compression maps by first-coordinate projection:

\[
m_1(a,b)=a,
\qquad
m_2(a,b)=a.
\]

Define one admissible probe

\[
I:B_1\to B_2,
\qquad
I(a,b)=(b,0).
\]

Its response is

\[
\mathcal R_I(a,b)
:=m_2(I(a,b))
=b.
\]

Now choose

\[
z=(0,0),
\qquad
z'=(0,1).
\]

Then

\[
m_1(z)=m_1(z')=0,
\]

but

\[
\mathcal R_I(z)=0,
\qquad
\mathcal R_I(z')=1.
\]

Hence

\[
J(z)\ne J(z').
\]

This model uses no quaternion algebra, no temporal order, no terminal object, no future boundary condition, and no consciousness claim.

It proves that the local birth statement is mathematically consistent and model-independent.

### What this witness does not prove

The model is intentionally minimal. It does not yet supply:

- a meaningful representative composition \(\Vert\);
- a response-profile product law;
- a nontrivial residual-depth hierarchy;
- a final standalone equality;
- a construction \(\lambda\mapsto z_\lambda\);
- the quaternionic recovery sector;
- the full Free Numbers algebra.

It proves independence of the phenomenon, not completion of the theory.

## 5. Status of the symbol \(0[\lambda]\)

The notation

\[

\]

is currently legitimate only as shorthand for a zero-valued state with a response profile associated with \(\lambda\).

It is not yet a defined algebraic construction because no accepted map

\[
\lambda\longmapsto z_\lambda\in Z_n
\]

has been established.

The theory must not infer mathematical distinction from labels alone. A parameter \(\lambda\) earns structural status only through at least one of:

- a specified representative realization;
- a specified deformation operator;
- a grade-changing extension;
- a relational constraint;
- an observable response difference.

Until then, \(0[\lambda]\) is a target notation, not a primitive fact.

## 6. Quaternionic model: evidence, not definition

In the quaternionic boundary-word model,

\[
B_2=V\otimes V,
\qquad
\ker m_2=S^2_0V.
\]

A nonzero element

\[
S\in S^2_0V
\]

satisfies

\[
m_2(S)=0
\]

while the established insertion response satisfies

\[
\mathcal R_2^{(1)}(S)=-2S.
\]

Thus the model gives a nontrivial, highly structured realization of a zero-valued but response-visible representative.

However, this proves only that the quaternionic model realizes the response distinction. It does not yet prove that an abstract datum \(\lambda\) canonically selects \(S\), nor that every conditioned-zero phenomenon is quaternionic.

The correct layer relation is therefore:

\[
\text{abstract response distinction}
\quad\longrightarrow\quad
\text{quaternionic realization candidate},
\]

not

\[
\text{conditioned zero by definition}=S^2_0V.
\]
